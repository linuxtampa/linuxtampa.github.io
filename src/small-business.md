# For Small Business Owners

If you're a small business owner in the **Tampa or Lakeland, FL** area, this page is for you.

I'm Tim Bailey Jones — a software engineer and Linux consultant with 30+ years of experience. I mainly work with enterprise clients, but I also genuinely enjoy helping small businesses get more out of their technology without handing all their data and budget to BigTech.

---

## What I Can Help You With

### Get Your Cloud & SaaS Costs Under Control

Monthly subscriptions add up fast. Many small businesses are paying for tools they don't fully use, or paying cloud prices for workloads that could run cheaper on a simple server. I can audit what you're spending, find the waste, and help you right-size — or get out entirely.

### Take Back Control of Your Data

When your files live in Google Drive, your email is in Microsoft 365, and your customer data is in some SaaS platform you barely understand, you don't really own your business data — they do. I help businesses move to self-hosted alternatives where your data stays on your hardware, in your building, under your control.

Popular options I can set up and support:

- **[Nextcloud](https://nextcloud.com)** — your own Google Drive / Dropbox, on your server
- **[Odoo](https://odoo.com)** — full business software (CRM, invoicing, inventory, HR) without per-seat fees
- **[Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com)** — self-hosted password management for your whole team
- **Security cameras & NVR** — own your footage, no cloud subscription, no monthly fees

### AI Tools — Without Sending Your Data to BigTech

AI can genuinely help small businesses work faster. But most AI tools send everything you type to servers you don't control. I can help you:

- Put AI tools to practical use in your day-to-day workflow
- Set up **private, local AI** that runs on your own hardware — your customer data, contracts, and internal documents never leave your building
- Add AI features to your existing website or application
