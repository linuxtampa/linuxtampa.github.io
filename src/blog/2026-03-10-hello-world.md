After more than three decades writing software for large organizations, I recently made the leap to independent consulting under the banner of **LinuxTampa, LLC**.

This wasn't entirely by choice — the W-2 market has been brutal lately, and I'm far from alone in that experience. But the more I've leaned into contract work, the more I've realized it suits me. I get to pick interesting problems, work with a wider range of technology stacks, and spend less time in status meetings.

## What LinuxTampa Is

LinuxTampa is my vehicle for helping businesses get real value out of open source technology and cloud infrastructure. The name reflects where I live and what I've built my career on: Linux, in all its forms, from embedded systems to enterprise cloud.

Over the years I've worked across a wide range of domains — telecommunications, financial services, defense, robotics, AI validation. The common thread has been Linux, automation, and a preference for building things that actually last.
